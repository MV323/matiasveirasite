About

Meet Matias

Matias Veira, CPA

With over a decade of experience in public and private accounting, Matias founded Matias Veira Accounting to give growing businesses access to the same caliber of financial expertise that large corporations take for granted.

His approach is hands-on and personal. Every client gets direct access to Matias — not a junior associate, not a call center. This means faster answers, deeper understanding of your business, and financial guidance that actually moves the needle.

Prior to launching his own practice, Matias held senior roles in accounting and finance, performing accounting services in biotech, energy, construction and SaaS services.
